BWM Machinery

B.W.Mack (Machinery) Ltd are based in Downham Market, Norfolk. We are main agents for Claas tractor and green harvest equipment, Merlo telehandlers, Vogel & Noot, Lely, Hardi and A.S Marston trailers. We trade with many other suppliers and have direct access to other types of machinery. We are also Claas service agents providing service and parts support to our local Claas combine customers.
